What is SBTI?

SBTI (Shabi Type Indicator) — a purely comedic personality quiz. Not scientific, but hilarious.

Origin Story

SBTI was created in April 2025 by Chinese content creator @qu-rou-er-chuan-er on Bilibili. It was originally made to convince a friend to drink less, but unexpectedly went viral across Chinese social media — from Bilibili to Xiaohongshu, Douyin to Weibo.

Key Features

31 cleverly designed questions covering 5 models — Self, Emotion, Attitude, Action, and Social — with 3 dimensions each, matching you to one of 27 soul types. Takes about 2 minutes, completely free.

Five Models

Self Model (Self-worth / Self-clarity / Core Values), Emotion Model (Attachment Security / Emotional Investment / Boundaries), Attitude Model (Worldview / Rules & Flexibility / Sense of Meaning), Action Model (Motivation / Decision Style / Execution), Social Model (Initiative / Boundaries / Expression & Authenticity).

Important Disclaimer

SBTI has absolutely zero scientific basis. It's not meant to diagnose your personality — it's meant to make you laugh at yourself. If the result doesn't seem accurate, that's the point. But if it's funny, it's worth it.

FAQ

Things you might want to know, and things we want to explain.

Is the SBTI result accurate?
No! SBTI has zero scientific basis. But accuracy isn't the point — having fun is. If you think the result is spot-on, it means you have great self-awareness (or you're just really good at relating to things).
How long does the test take?
About 2-3 minutes, with 31 questions total. Completely free, no registration, no phone number required. Take it and go.
Is my data uploaded anywhere?
No. All calculations happen locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. We don't even have a server.
I heard there are hidden types?
That's right! 2 of the 27 types are special: "The Drunkard" (DRUNK) is only triggered by answering specific questions about drinking, and "The Happy Fool" (HHHH) is a fallback type when your answers don't closely match any standard type.
Who created SBTI?
SBTI was originally created by Bilibili creator @qu-rou-er-chuan-er. This site is a mirror hosted on Cloudflare. All credit goes to the original creator!
Can I share my result?
Absolutely! Take a screenshot and share it on social media. Spreading joy is SBTI's only mission.
